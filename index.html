Grading

This assessment involves troubleshooting the circuit which immitates a real circuit made up of real parts.

  • All the assessments combinedly constitute 10% of your total grade.
  • Each assessment has an unlimited number of tries and no time-limit.
  • There is no penalty for getting a question wrong.
  • Your best score out of all your attempts is used to calculate your grade.
